Project Academic

About Project Academic

Providing a service to school students by supplying them with research essays shining the light on the relevant information by simplifying and displaying complicated and specific information in a more brief and straightforward approach.

Our Goals

Approaching local publishing companies, writers, researchers, and inviting them to publish their valuable information gathered on our website in exchange for exposure to their brand/name which might elevate into a percentage cut in case we decide to offer a subscription to users/students. Possibly expanding in a more global way in the next few years by notifying potential contributors from different ethnicities.
